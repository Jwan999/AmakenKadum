Our Story

Amakin is an individual project.
It's purpose is to deliver an image about the places that deserve to be seen, visited and known
The places which haven't got what it deserves, we will deliver a picture about them and let you see them through our eyes
Those places could be museums, traditional markets, normal shops, restaurants, malls, cafes, public or private projects! whether
It's a normal or a fancy place they all have their stories and a spirituality of its own which deserves to be talked about, thus you'd
See Iraq from a different scope reflecting the beauty of every place we visit.
